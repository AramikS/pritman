What is polishing? It is the removal of the thinnest layers of varnish, allowing you to eliminate scratches and achieve a smooth and shiny surface.

An ideal addition would be the application of ceramic coating, which fills in all the micro-gaps and provides an incredible smoothness to the varnish. It also repels dust, dirt, and water. This means that the car will get less dirty and be easier to wash during subsequent cleanings.

Treating a car body with wax forms a film on the surface of the car body that protects the surface from moisture, small stones and other environmental factors. It's worse than ceramics but cheaper.
